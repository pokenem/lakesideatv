FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • At what age can you ride alone?

    From 16 years old.

  • Do you have protective gear?

    We provide a helmet and under-helmet, foot protection. This is included in the ride cost.

  • Can two people ride on one ATV?

    Yes.

  • Can women drive a large ATV?

    Yes, women can drive an ATV.

  • I have never ridden before, do I need to pay extra for training?

    No extra payment is required for training.

  • Is the ATV automatic or manual?

    The ATV has an automatic transmission, no need to shift gears.

  • What happens if the ATV breaks down from a fall or collision?

    It must be remembered that you must return the equipment to the rental point in good condition. Upon arrival at the base after the tour, our employee will inspect the equipment for breakdowns. In case of damage, you will have to pay for the broken part.

  • The ATV ride participant agrees that the maximum speed will be limited to 40 km/h for safety reasons.
  • Only individuals aged 16 and above are allowed to ride adult ATVs.

  • ATV rides take place only in the presence of an instructor.
  • A briefing on ATV operation must be completed before use.
  • Do not participate in races or collisions that may cause damage to property and other participants.

PROHIBITED

  • Riding ATVs while intoxicated or under the influence of drugs.
  • Deliberately driving into obstacles.
  • Deliberately colliding with other ATVs.
  • Riding without the accompaniment of an instructor.
